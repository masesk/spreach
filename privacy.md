# Spreach by Mases Krikorian – Privacy Policy

*Last updated: November 16, 2025*

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how Spreach collects, stores, and uses information.

---

## 1. Overview

Spreach is designed with privacy in mind. All text‑to‑speech processing occurs **entirely on your device**. The app does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Spreach does **not**:

* Collect personal information.
* Store text you input.
* Upload audio or text to servers.
* Track usage analytics.
* Use third‑party trackers or advertising SDKs.

---

## 3. Local Processing

All text‑to‑speech operations run locally using your device’s CPU or GPU. No content leaves your device during generation.

---

## 4. Device Permissions

Spreach may request the following permissions:

* **Storage access** (to save generated audio files).
* **Microphone or media permissions** (optional features, if enabled).

These permissions are used only for their stated purpose and remain under your control.

---

## 5. In-App Purchases and Trial Data

### 5.1 Trial Information

When you start your 30-day free trial, the trial start date is stored **locally on your device** using the iOS Keychain. This information:

* Remains on your device only
* Is not transmitted to any external servers
* Is used solely to determine trial eligibility

### 5.2 Purchase Information

When you purchase Spreach Premium through the App Store:

* Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple
* Mases Krikorian does not collect, process, or store your payment information
* Apple may provide transaction receipts to verify your purchase, which are validated locally on your device
* No personal payment data is accessible to or stored by Spreach

Purchase verification is performed using Apple's StoreKit 2 framework and occurs on your device.

---

## 6. Third‑Party Services

Spreach does not integrate with external analytics, cloud services, or advertising networks. The Kokoro 82M model is packaged within the app and runs locally.

**Apple App Store**: Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store, which is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

---

## 7. Data Security

Since user data is not collected or transmitted, risk is minimized. Any files you create remain stored on your device, under your control.

Trial start dates stored in the iOS Keychain benefit from Apple's hardware-level security protections.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Spreach does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe information has been inadvertently collected, contact us immediately.

---

## 9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app or on masesk.com.
